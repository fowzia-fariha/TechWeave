Creating an account is simple:

TechWeave offers several features:

  • Learning Paths: Access structured courses and tutorials
  • Chat System: Connect with mentors and peers in real-time
  • Group Discussions: Join the General Chat to discuss with everyone
  • Mentor Support: Get personalized guidance from experienced mentors

üí¨ Chat & Messaging

How do I join the General Chat? ‚ñº

When you create an account, you're automatically added to the General Chat! Simply:

  • Go to Dashboard
  • Click "Chat with Community"
  • Select "Groups" tab
  • Click on "General Chat"
  • Start messaging!
How do I send a private message to a mentor? ‚ñº

To chat privately with a mentor:

  • Go to the Chat page
  • Click on the "Mentors" tab
  • You'll see all available mentors with a green "Mentor" badge
  • Click on any mentor's name
  • Type your message and press Send
  • Only you and the mentor can see these messages
Can other students see my private messages with mentors? ‚ñº

No! Private messages are completely confidential. Only you and the mentor you're chatting with can see the conversation. Other students cannot access your private messages.

What's the difference between Group Chat and Private Chat? ‚ñº

Group Chat (General Chat):

  • Everyone in the group can see all messages
  • Great for general discussions and community engagement
  • All students and mentors can participate

Private Chat:

  • Only you and the mentor can see messages
  • Perfect for personal questions and guidance
  • One-on-one conversation
Are my chat messages saved? ‚ñº

Yes! All messages (both group and private) are saved in the database. When you return to a chat, you'll see your entire conversation history. Messages persist even after you log out or refresh the page.

How do I find a mentor? ‚ñº

Finding a mentor is easy:

Yes! If you have experience and want to help others, you can apply to become a mentor. Contact the admin team through the support email, and they'll review your application. Mentors are selected based on their expertise and willingness to help students.

What kind of help can mentors provide? ‚ñº

Mentors can help with:

  • Programming questions and code reviews
  • Career guidance and advice
  • Project suggestions and feedback
  • Learning path recommendations
  • Technical problem-solving
  • Best practices and industry insights
How quickly do mentors respond? ‚ñº

Response times vary by mentor availability. Most mentors try to respond within a few hours. Remember that mentors are volunteers helping in their free time. Be patient and respectful of their time!

Can I change my username? ‚ñº

Currently, username changes are not available through the interface. If you need to change your username, please contact support with your current username and desired new username.

If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team at support@techweave.com. Note that this action is permanent and will delete all your messages, progress, and data.

Is my data secure on TechWeave? ‚ñº

Yes! We take security seriously:

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt
  • JWT tokens for secure authentication
  • Private messages are only accessible by participants
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

üîß Technical Issues

My messages aren't sending. What's wrong? ‚ñº

If messages aren't sending, try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Look for the connection status (should show "Connected" in green)
  • Refresh the page (F5)
  • Log out and log back in
  • Clear your browser cache
  • If problem persists, contact support
The chat shows "Disconnected". How do I fix this? ‚ñº

If you see a "Disconnected" status:

  • Check if the backend server is running
  • Verify your internet connection
  • Refresh the page
  • Try logging out and back in
  • Check if port 5000 is accessible
Which browsers are supported? ‚ñº

TechWeave works best on:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari (latest version)

For best experience, keep your browser updated to the latest version.

Can I use TechWeave on mobile? ‚ñº

Yes! TechWeave is mobile-responsive. You can access it through your mobile browser. For the best mobile experience, use Chrome or Safari on your phone.

I'm getting a "Please login first" error ‚ñº

This happens when your session expires or you're not logged in. To fix:

  • Go to the Login page
  • Enter your credentials
  • If you can't login, try resetting your password
  • Make sure cookies are enabled in your browser
